SDG 3 — Good Health SDG 11 — Sustainable Cities SDG 13 — Climate Action

Over 300 million people live with asthma. Millions more face daily respiratory distress from urban pollution, anxiety, and environmental exposure — with no real-time, personalized support at the moment they need it most.

BreatheSafe addresses this gap by combining live environmental data, AI-powered guidance, and an emergency recovery experience — built to help users feel safe, calm, and in control of their breathing.
